About Us

MakeMyQRCode was created to provide a simple, free, and permanent QR code generator for users around the world.

Our mission is to make QR code creation:

  • Free for everyone
  • Easy to use
  • Reliable and non-expiring

We believe QR codes should never stop working or require subscriptions.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted free QR code generator worldwide, offering lifetime QR codes without complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do QR codes generated on MakeMyQRCode expire?

No. All QR codes created on makemyqrcode.com are non-expiring and permanent. Once generated, your QR code will continue to work forever as long as the linked URL remains active.

2. Are the QR codes really free to use?

Yes. MakeMyQRCode is 100% free in PNG version. You can generate unlimited QR codes without signing up, without subscriptions, and without hidden charges.

3. What type of QR codes can I create?

You can generate QR codes for any web link, including:

  • - Websites
  • - Landing pages
  • - Social media profiles
  • - Google Maps links
  • - Online forms and documents

Simply paste your URL and generate your QR code instantly.

4. Can I customize the color of my QR code?

Absolutely. You can change the QR code colors and background based on your design or brand preferences before downloading.

5. In which format can I download the QR code?

You can download your QR code as a high-quality PNG file, suitable for both digital use and printing on posters, visiting cards, packaging, and banners. For SVG file you have to registered your profile.

6. Is registration or login required?

No registration is required for PNG file. You can generate and download QR codes instantly without creating an account.

7. Will my QR code work worldwide?

Yes. QR codes generated on makemyqrcode.com work globally and can be scanned by any QR scanner or smartphone camera anywhere in the world.

8. Are these QR codes safe to use for business?

Yes. Our QR codes are reliable and perfect for:

  • - Business cards
  • - Product packaging
  • - Restaurant menus
  • - Events and promotions
  • - Marketing campaigns

They do not expire and do not stop working.

9. Can I use these QR codes for commercial purposes?

Yes. You are free to use the generated QR codes for both personal and commercial use without any restrictions.

10. Do you add watermarks to the QR codes?

No. All downloaded QR codes are clean, watermark-free, and ready to use.

11. What happens if the linked URL changes?

QR codes store the link you provide. If the URL changes or is removed, the QR code will stop working. Make sure the linked URL remains active.
