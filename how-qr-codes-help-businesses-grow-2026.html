Static vs Dynamic QR Codes

Understanding the difference helps you choose the right type:

Static QR Codes

  • Fixed content (cannot be edited later)
  • Best for permanent information
  • No tracking available
  • Free and simple to use

Example: Website URL or basic contact info

Dynamic QR Codes

  • Editable anytime without changing the QR code
  • Track scans, location, and performance
  • Ideal for marketing campaigns
  • More flexible and scalable

Example: Running offers, campaigns, or changing links
